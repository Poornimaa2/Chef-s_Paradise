About Us

What do you want to know?

Chef's paradise brings you the magic of fine dining restaurant to your table, through talented chefs who will build a menu to your cravings. Enjoy a customized culinary experience with a Private Chef in the comfort of your own sweet homes!

Your personal chef can provide you with the following:
  • Weekly meals that fit your dietary and nutritional needs.
  • Fine in-home dining for special events for dates, parties, etc.
  • Food options for seniors and those with special diets.
  • Food from your chosen cuisine.
  • Cooking lessons.
  • Vacation home cooking
  • Vacation meal preparation.
